How to Invest

  1. Register or login to your account.
  2. Choose your investment tier and amount.
  3. Agree to the terms and confirm investment.
  4. Make a payment via M-Pesa . bank transfer (coming soon).
  5. Track your investment progress on your dashboard.
  6. Receive your principal and interest on maturity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my principal guaranteed?

Yes! Amsa guarantees the return of your principal investment.

Can I withdraw before maturity?

Currently, early withdrawal is not supported to protect investment stability.

How do I track my investment?

Simply log in and visit your investor dashboard to see real-time progress.
