About Amsa Group

Amsa is a duly registered investment company in Kenya, specializing in secure, interest-earning investments. We are a robust investment platform with high yield returns paid weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annually, serving all cadres of investors with a mission to make wealth creation accessible through a trustworthy client-centric fund with full regulatory compliance.

Our Mission

To provide accessible micro-investment opportunities fostering financial independence and growth to investing communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform in Kenya for secure and reliable micro-investment solutions whilst transforming lives through investment empowerment.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency.
  • Empowerment: We empower our clients with wealth creation.
  • Community: We build strong communities around shared success.
  • Innovation: We embrace technology to reach the underserved investing communities.
