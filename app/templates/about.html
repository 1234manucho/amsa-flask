About Amsa Group

Amsa Group is a duly registered investment and land development company in Kenya, specializing in secure, interest-earning investment opportunities and affordable land ownership solutions. We offer flexible installment payment plans for land buyers and transparent investment contracts with fixed interest returns paid weekly, monthly, quarterly, and beyond, serving both local and diaspora clients. Our mission is to make wealth creation and property acquisition accessible and trustworthy through legally compliant and client-centric services.
